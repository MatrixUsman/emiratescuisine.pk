# Quick Wins - Easy Improvements to Implement

These are high-impact, easy-to-implement features that will significantly improve your website.

## 1. WhatsApp Click-to-Chat Button ⭐⭐⭐
**Impact**: Very High | **Effort**: Low

Add a floating WhatsApp button that opens chat directly.

## 2. Phone Number (Click-to-Call) ⭐⭐⭐
**Impact**: Very High | **Effort**: Low

Add phone numbers in header/footer with click-to-call on mobile.

## 3. Opening Hours Display ⭐⭐⭐
**Impact**: High | **Effort**: Low

Show restaurant hours on homepage and branches page.

## 4. Social Media Links ⭐⭐
**Impact**: Medium | **Effort**: Low

Add Facebook, Instagram, TikTok icons in footer/header.

## 5. Back to Top Button ⭐⭐
**Impact**: Medium | **Effort**: Low

Floating button to scroll to top on long pages.

## 6. Menu Search Functionality ⭐⭐⭐
**Impact**: High | **Effort**: Medium

Search bar to filter menu items by name.

## 7. Testimonials Section ⭐⭐⭐
**Impact**: High | **Effort**: Medium

3-5 customer reviews with ratings on homepage.

## 8. Food Gallery Section ⭐⭐
**Impact**: Medium | **Effort**: Medium

Showcase section with best food photos.

## 9. FAQ Section ⭐⭐
**Impact**: Medium | **Effort**: Low

Common questions about restaurant, menu, delivery.

## 10. About Us Page ⭐⭐
**Impact**: Medium | **Effort**: Medium

Restaurant story, mission, values.

---

**Start with #1-5** - They can be implemented in under 30 minutes each and provide immediate value!
